Are web pages characterized by color ? When human guess the content of a web page , not only the text on the page but also its appearance is an important factor . However , there have been few studies on the relationshipbetween the content and visual appearance of a web page . We investigating the tendencybetween them , especially web content and color use , we found a tendency to use color for some kinds of content pages . We think this result opens the way to estimating web content using color information .
