Semantic resource management for the web : an e-learning application Topics in education are changing with an ever faster pace . ELearning resources tend to be more and more decentralized . Users increasingly need to be able to use the resources of the web . For this , they should have tools for finding and organizing information in a decentralized way . In this paper , we show how an ontologybased tool suite allows to make the most of the resources available on the web .
